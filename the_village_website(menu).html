Jollof Rice $12.99

Jollof rice is a beloved West African dish with many regional variations. It's made with a unique blend of spices, long-grain rice, and a tomato and red pepper base. It is often served as a hearty entrée by adding beef, chicken, goat, or fish, or as is as a side..

Pounded Yam $11.99

Smooth and delicious pounded yam, a perfect side dish for your meal. Pounded Yam is a popular African dish similar to mashed potatoes but heavier. Pounded Yam is very smooth and tasty. It is often eaten with vegetable soup.

Chin Chin $14.99

Crispy and sweet fried dough bites, a delightful snack for any time. Chin-chin is a popular Nigerian snack made from a stiff paste. The dough is made with flour, butter, milk, eggs, and other ingredients. The dough is cut into shapes and sizes, then deep-fried or baked until crispy. Chin-chin can be eaten with tea or coffee, or on its own.

Egusi Soup $10.99

Egusi soup is a spicy, protein-rich West African soup. It's made with a red base, melon seeds, and greens. The red base is made from a blend of peppers, including habanero,and bell, also assorted meats.. The soup gets its name from the egusi seeds, which thicken and flavor it.
